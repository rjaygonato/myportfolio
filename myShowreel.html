myShowreal

I built this site as a way to showcase what I actually do—not just tell people.

Over time, I’ve worked on creating short-form content for products and affiliate marketing, especially for platforms like Shopee and social media. I realized that while most portfolios just list skills or show static images, they don’t really capture the impact of the work—how videos grab attention, keep people watching, and drive clicks.

So I decided to create a space where I can present my work in a more real and engaging way. This site is a collection of the projects, videos, and ideas I’ve worked on, focused on one goal: creating content that converts, not just looks good.
